Diet Plan for Weight Loss

Achieving your weight loss goals requires a balanced diet plan that nourishes your body while helping you shed extra pounds. Our expert nutritionists recommend:

  • High Protein Foods: Lean meats, legumes, and tofu to support muscle recovery.
  • Whole Grains: Brown rice, oats, and quinoa for sustained energy.
  • Fruits and Vegetables: Low-calorie foods rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Hydration: Drink at least 2 liters of water daily to stay hydrated and support metabolism.
  • Healthy Fats: Avocado, olive oil, and nuts in moderation.
